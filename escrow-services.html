Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose a trusted and secure title company?

Choosing a trusted and secure title company ensures that your real estate transaction is handled with professionalism and integrity. They have the expertise to navigate complex legal and regulatory requirements, protect your interests, and safeguard the funds and documents involved in the transaction.

What is a title search, and why is it necessary?

A title search is a thorough examination of public records to verify the property's ownership history and uncover any liens, encumbrances, or potential issues that may affect the title's validity. It is necessary to ensure that the buyer receives a clear and marketable title.

How does title insurance protect property buyers?

Title insurance protects property buyers by providing coverage against financial losses or legal issues related to the property's title. It helps protect against unforeseen claims, such as undisclosed liens, errors in public records, or fraudulent ownership claims.
